Event Registration

Go to the Event Registration section on the home page. Fill in your name, email, preferred date, and select an event type, then click Register Now. You will see a confirmation message on screen.
Yes. Simply fill in the registration form again for each event you wish to attend. Each submission is treated separately.
Make sure all required fields (marked with an asterisk) are filled in correctly. Check that your email address is in a valid format and that you have selected an event type from the dropdown.

Preferences

When you select a preferred event type in the Your Preferences section, it is saved in your browser's local storage. The next time you visit the portal, your preference will be automatically pre-selected for you.
Click the Clear Preferences button in the Preferences section. This will remove all saved data from your browser for this portal.

Finding Nearby Events

The Find Nearby Events feature uses your device's GPS to show you events happening close to where you are. Your location is only used in the browser and is never stored or sent to any server.
If you deny permission, the portal will show a message letting you know. You can still browse and register for events manually without sharing your location.

Feedback & Contact

Scroll to the Event Feedback section. Enter your phone number, select the event, write your feedback, and click Submit Feedback.
Use the Contact Us section at the bottom of the home page. Fill in your name, email, subject, and message and click Send Message. You can also call us at +91 44 2345 6789 or email events@citycouncil.gov.in.
