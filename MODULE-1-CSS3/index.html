Community Bulletin

The city council has announced a month-long community participation drive. Residents are encouraged to join neighborhood-level activities focused on cleanliness, public health awareness, and weekend recreation. Each ward will host at least one skill-sharing session where local volunteers can teach photography, basic first aid, spoken English, and arts for children. Organizers have requested residents to complete event registration forms early so that seating and safety arrangements can be managed smoothly.
